Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. Accordingly, we have developed this Policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate and disclose and make use of personal information. 
he policy concerns GeekTyper.com. The following outlines our privacy policy.

Who are we?
GeekTyper.com is a service providing a tool to change the appearance of your Windows, Mac Desktop, iPhone or iPad Homescreen. It is not a data-centric entity and we collect as little data about our users as possible. 
The only data we collect is instrumental to the site’s core functionality – such as user accounts, skins database, ratings system and subscription status.

What information do we collect?
The following information is being collected:
> When you use Steam to login on GeekTyper.com, your profile details are mirrored to create an account here.
> These profile details are provided by Steam’s OAuth and include only your account ID, Username, Tagline and Profile Picture (information such as email, passwords, profile details are not transferred and not available).
> When you create skins and submit them, they are also stored on our servers unless you delete them
> Star ratings and subscription status is stored on our servers
> Payment information is not recorded or stored, as the payment goes through PayPal, please consult their privacy policy for further details.
> Statistics may be collected using Google Analytics with respect to GDPR laws

How do we use personal information?
> Identification of your unique user profile and the skins made by you
> Account set up and administration
> Usage statistics and demographics
> Providing software as a service

When do we share personal data?
We do not share your data with 3rd parties directly. The content of the website is indexed automatically by Google Analytics for:
> Usage Statistics and Demographics

Where do we store and process personal data?
Your account on GeekTyper.com, your skins, ratings and subscription status exist in a database in New York City, on a server managed by Digital Ocean.

How do we secure personal data?
The following security measures are taken to ensure the safety and integrity of your data:
> Encrypted Backups to prevent data loss
> Secure credentials to prevent unauthorised access, use, destruction or disclosure
> Encryption to restrict access to personal information
> End to end HTTPS encryption
> Measures to conduct privacy impact assessments in accordance with the law
> Staff training on digital security
> The administrators of the site cannot see or decode the account passwords.
> Login details (passwords) are salted in the case of an extremely unlikely data breach.

How long do we keep your personal data for?
Your data is stored on our servers for no longer than necessary. As you publish skins, they will reside on the site for as long as you desire them to be online and / or for as long as this service operates.

Upon deletion of your skins or your account (termination of the account also deletes your skins), the information is removed from the server.

Your rights in relation to personal data

> You may delete your account and all its associated data
> You may set your Profile as private, or yourself Username as Anonymous
